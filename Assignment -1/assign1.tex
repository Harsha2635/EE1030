Consider $f(x)=ke^x-x$ for all real x where k is a real constant.
\begin{enumerate}
\item The line $y=x$ meets $y=ke^x$ for $k \le 0$ at

\item no point
\item one point
\item two points
\item more than two points
\end{enumerate}
